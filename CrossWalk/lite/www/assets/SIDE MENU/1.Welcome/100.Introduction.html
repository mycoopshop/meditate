Welcome to Mindfulness: The Art of Being Human. How are you feeling right now? Excited to try something new? Or maybe just curious to explore?

What is this?

This app is the multimedia version of the book Mindfulness: The Art of Being Human. This book is currently only available in this app. Unlike a standard book, this is broken down into bite-sized sections that you can explore at will. It also comes with guided audio tracks and video.

Mindfulness is purely an experience. Simply reading the words of a book will never get you to the actual experience of it. This app includes a book on the human mind and behaviour, not also experiential exercises of guided audio tracks and exercises, in order to provide an opportunity to experience mindfulness.

How can I use this?

But go easy on yourself. I want you to enjoy this app. So, here's 3 ways in which you can use it:

1) Simply explore the app by dipping into the content as you wish (in the Knowledge section and following the audio in the Experience section).

2) Start the 8 Day Challenge (in the Challenge section)

3) Start the 8 Day Micro Course (also in the Challenge section)

The main part of this app is...

...broken down into two main sections: Knowledge (the book) and Experience (the exercises).

  • Knowledge comprises the complete text of the book. It explores insights into the human mind and patterns of behaviour.
  • Experience is the practical part of the app. It contains a set of guided meditations to practise. It will guide you through some of the concepts and ideas explored in Knowledge.

If you simply want to get on with practising mindfulness, then start with the Experience section.

If you want to explore the app more thoroughly, you might find that you learn a lot more than you expected. When we have a little insight into the human mind, behaviour and the practice of mindfulness, it can have a powerful effect on helping us live more open, receptive and meaningful lives.

SUMMARY

- Resistance is natural. It's not a problem in moderation. But when used to excess, it can cause long-term problems.

- When we’re resisting, we’re often distracting ourselves from a situation or taking control of it. Both are a way of trying to control how we feel.

- Our thoughts and feelings can sometimes pull us away from the things that matter. This can lead to a conflict within us.

- People don't need fixing. We can simply fall into quick fix behaviours and get stuck in cycles of resistance.

- The first step towards change is awareness. The second step is acceptance. Mindfulness is both these steps.

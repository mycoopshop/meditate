I want you to know something. It would be easy to become part of an anti-mind brigade having read the material above! Our minds, being a process of information, are essential for our survival, evolution and everyday living. However, many of our thoughts aren’t useful. But by practising mindfulness, we have more of a capacity to choose the thoughts that are in alignment with who we really are or what we really want to do.

So, if you're ready and willing, let’s dive into this journey of exploring the human mind, behaviour and the practice of mindfulness.

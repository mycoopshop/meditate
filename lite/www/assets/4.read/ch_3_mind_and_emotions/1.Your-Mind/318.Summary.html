SUMMARY

- Your brain has been designed to be active and on guard from any threat. The mind follows accordingly.

- Most people hardly use their minds at all. Their minds use them.

- The mind is a process of information.

- The mind judges and evaluates anything and everything, including the very thoughts that it created in the first place.

- Over half of our thoughts could be perceived to be negative or unhelpful.

- The mind’s 'fix-it' problem-solving approach doesn’t work when it sets about solving unwanted thoughts and feelings.

- The nervous system works with an input-only function. There’s no delete button for thoughts.

- You are not your mind. Mindfulness allows you to observe this.

SUMMARY

- If we observe thoughts and feelings with mindfulness, we are less influenced by them.

- We can view thoughts as associations or 'solutions' to the 'problem' of how we're feeling.

- It’s not our thoughts, but our attachment to our thoughts, that causes suffering.

- It’s the relationship we have with our thoughts that either frees us up or keeps us imprisoned.

- Resisting or buying into unwanted thoughts provides them with more energy. Mindfulness is the third way of responding, which does neither.

- Let your thoughts float and you won't take them so seriously.

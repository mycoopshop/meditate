Phase two - Exploring

This is about turning towards some of the experiences you would normally turn away from. It's about being a little more flexible with how you relate to yourself. In this case, it can simply mean observing with kind awareness the resistance that you’re feeling, so as not to become consumed in it.

Self-awareness is an act of self-kindness.

Even if observing is all you can do in that moment, that’s okay. While you observe it, you’re not identified with it. You're not feeding it. This is an act of self-kindness, even if it doesn’t feel like it.

If we don't notice and tune into ourselves, we can be entangled with inner resistance and even unaware of it. Without this awareness, we’ll continually be in the struggle. If we continue with this struggle, our relationship with similar feelings or thoughts will always and only be one of resistance.

If you are never willing, you are always unwilling; always in a state of resistance …

So, wherever and whenever …

Take a moment to tune into yourself.

Pay attention to any striving to do things or urges to be elsewhere.

Take gentle awareness to these and tune into any inner resistance you may notice.

Tune in and acknowledge any tension you may be aware of.

See if you can create a wave of non-resistance flowing through your body as you release it.

If not, simply observe this. Be curious.

Let it be, and continue to be the witness to this.

The 4 major benefits to the art of allowing

This video is available in the full version of this app.

1) It provides for us a space to know how we are emotionally, before being made aware of our feelings when they're at a greater intensity,

2) It gives us the insight to recognise that, in those moments of awareness, our emotions are just an experience. A feeling that doesn't have to influence our behaviour and be associated with life in the external world, and

3) In this space is the capacity to choose. The choice point of being under the influence of unwanted feelings or being the witness to these as they arise.

4) The thickening of neural pathways in the brain associated to this. The more we practise, the more our brain adapts and grows for this very purpose. This helps strengthen our tendency to be able to do this for future situations.

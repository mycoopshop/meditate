This is a short and simple meditation for breathing awareness. It is also the mindfulness track suitable for the 8 Day Challenge, if you are doing the challenge for just 10 minutes a day, instead of 20.

The breath is fascinating. Did you know that we take an average of 19,000 breaths each day? Rarely do we give any of them our full attention. Yet the natural flow of our breathing has been with us from birth.

Breath is the bridge which connects life to consciousness, which unites your body to your thoughts. Whenever your mind becomes scattered, use your breath as the means to take hold of your mind again. - Thich Nhat Hanh

The more we practise this, the easier is is to refrain from behaviours that don't serve us. We'll explore this within the Knowledge section.
